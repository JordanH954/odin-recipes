Curry Chicken

Description:

Curry Chicken is a classic caribbean dish that is easy to make, delicious, and satiating. There are many ways to make curry chicken depending on the culture. It's a timeless dish that I grew up absolutely devouring.

Ingredients:

Curry Chicken is made with the following ingredients:

  • 15 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 1 small red onion
  • 3 medium scallions
  • 1 small habanero or scotch bonnet pepper
  • And a multitude of other ingredients!

Steps:
  1. Prepare the seasonings, meats, and utensils
  2. Pour all the ingredients into the pot
  3. Cook for the right amount of time.
